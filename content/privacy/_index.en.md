**Effective:** 17 July 2026

This policy describes how **LemricFit** (“the App”) handles data. LemricFit is built so that training data stays on your device. You do not need a LemricFit cloud account, and there is no mandatory server that stores your workouts.

## 1. Controller

Where GDPR applies to processing outside your exclusive control on the device, the controller is:

**Dominik Labudziński**

Contact: **[biuro@lemric.com](mailto:biuro@lemric.com)**

In practice, most profile and training data is stored **only on your device**, in the App’s encrypted database.

## 2. How the App works

1. You do not have to create an account with us.
2. Workouts, GPS tracks, sensor readings, goals, records, and settings live primarily in the encrypted database on the phone.
3. There is no social feed, clubs, or sharing between users inside LemricFit.
4. Network use is limited: maps, weather, place names, optional diagnostics, and optional system cloud backup — each for a clear purpose.

## 3. What data may be processed

### On the device (depending on what you use)

Profile details, workouts, GPS points, Bluetooth sensor readings, optional Health data (with system permission), photos, place name and weather at the end of an activity, app-lock PIN hash, recovery and encryption keys in Keychain/Keystore, encrypted backups, and preferences.

You do **not** need to give us an email address, phone number, or payment details to use core training features.

### Off the device (only in specific cases)

- Maps: Google Maps SDK on Android; Apple MapKit on iOS/macOS
- Place name: system geocoding
- Weather: Open-Meteo (coordinates for that request)
- Apple Health / Health Connect when you grant access
- Optional encrypted backup to iCloud or Google Drive app data (**without** the encryption key)
- Optional crash reporting (Sentry) only if that build includes a DSN; sensitive fields are stripped
- Anything you choose to share through the system share sheet

We do **not** sell your data and do **not** show behavioural ads.

## 4. Purposes and legal bases

Where GDPR applies: providing the App’s features; Health access with your consent; weather and place name for activity summaries; maps; optional cloud backup you turn on; optional diagnostics. You can revoke system permissions in Settings at any time.

## 5. Permissions

Location (including background while recording), Bluetooth, motion/activity recognition, Health, camera/photos, notifications, and biometrics for app lock — each only for the related feature.

## 6. Encryption and backups

The training database is encrypted. The key stays in Keychain/Keystore and is **not** packed into backup files. A cloud backup without that key is not usable alone. You can delete all local App data from inside LemricFit.

## 7. Retention

Local data until you delete it, uninstall the App, or wipe data in settings. Cloud backups last until you remove them. Health data follows Apple or Google rules. Map and weather providers apply their own policies to those requests.

## 8. Recipients

Platform providers (Apple, Google), Open-Meteo, Sentry (only if enabled in that build), and anyone you send content to when sharing. Their terms also apply.

## 9. Transfers outside the EEA

Some providers may process data outside the EEA under GDPR transfer tools. Using maps, weather, Health, Drive/iCloud, or optional Sentry means accepting that for the feature you use.

## 10. Your rights

Access, correction, erasure, restriction, portability, objection (where based on legitimate interest), withdrawing consent, and a complaint to a supervisory authority (in Poland: PUODO). Because most data is on the device, the practical way to erase it is: wipe data in the App, uninstall, and clear Health or cloud backups as needed.

For matters that need the controller (for example optional Sentry), email **[biuro@lemric.com](mailto:biuro@lemric.com)**.

## 11. Children

The App is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect children’s data.

## 12. Automated decisions

LemricFit does not make automated decisions with legal effects under GDPR Art. 22. Suggestions and stats are local aids.

## 13. Changes

We may update this policy when the App or the law changes. The effective date will change. Material updates will be noted in the App or store listing when practical.
