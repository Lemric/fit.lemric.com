## About LemricFit

LemricFit records GPS activities (run, ride, walk, hike) on your device. Optional Bluetooth sensors (heart rate, cadence, power) and Apple Health / Health Connect can enrich workouts. Training data stays on your phone — **no required cloud account**.

## Common questions

<div class="faq-grid">
  <div class="faq-item">
    <h3>Location / GPS</h3>
    <p>Location access is used to record your route during an activity. Change it anytime in system Settings. Without location, tracking cannot draw a GPS path.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="faq-item">
    <h3>Bluetooth sensors</h3>
    <p>Bluetooth connects heart-rate, cadence, and power sensors from the Sensors screen. Keep the sensor powered on and close to the phone while pairing.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="faq-item">
    <h3>Backups</h3>
    <p>Encrypted local backups are available in the app. Optional cloud backup (iCloud or Google Drive app data) never includes your encryption key. Keep your recovery key if you enable encryption features.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="faq-item">
    <h3>Account</h3>
    <p>LemricFit does not require creating an account with us. Your profile and workouts live on the device unless you choose optional backup or Health sync.</p>
  </div>
</div>
