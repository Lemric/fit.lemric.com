## About the app

LemricFit records GPS activities (run, ride, walk, hike) on your device. Optional Bluetooth sensors (heart rate, cadence, power) and Apple Health / Health Connect can enrich workouts. Training data stays on your phone — **no required cloud account**.

## Common questions

### Location / GPS

Location access is used to record your route during an activity. You can change this anytime in system Settings. Without location, tracking cannot draw a GPS path.

### Bluetooth sensors

Bluetooth is used to connect heart-rate, cadence, and power sensors. Pair them from the Sensors screen in the app. Make sure the sensor is powered on and close to the phone.

### Backups

Encrypted local backups are available in the app. Optional cloud backup (iCloud or Google Drive app data) never includes your encryption key. Keep your recovery key if you enable encryption features.

### Account

LemricFit does not require creating an account with us. Your profile and workouts live on the device unless you choose optional backup or Health sync.
